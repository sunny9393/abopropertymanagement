Our Core Values

1. Honesty & Integrity


Honesty and integrity are fundamental pillars for a property management company as they underpin trust, transparency, and long-term success. In the dynamic realm of property management, clients entrust these companies with the stewardship of valuable assets. Operating with unwavering honesty ensures clear communication, fostering strong relationships with property owners, tenants, and other stakeholders. Integrity in business practices builds a reputation for reliability and ethical conduct, instilling confidence in clients. Transparent dealings, truthful reporting, and ethical decision-making contribute to a positive and sustainable business environment. A property management company grounded in honesty and integrity not only meets legal and ethical obligations but also cultivates a culture of trust that is indispensable in this industry. This trust is the bedrock upon which enduring partnerships are built, enhancing the company's credibility and ensuring continued success in the competitive property management landscape.


2. Excellence Above All Else 


In the realm of property management and maintenance, achieving excellence is paramount for sustained success. A property management company that prioritizes excellence sets itself apart by delivering superior services in property upkeep, tenant satisfaction, and overall operational efficiency. Excellence in property management ensures that maintenance tasks are executed promptly and to the highest standards, utilizing advanced property management tools for optimal efficiency. This commitment to excellence enhances the property's value, attracts quality tenants, and mitigates potential issues before they escalate. Property owners benefit from the peace of mind that comes with entrusting their assets to a management company dedicated to excellence in every aspect of property care and tenant relations. Consistent excellence not only fosters client satisfaction but also establishes the property management company as a leader in the industry, driving organic growth and client referrals.

An Owner's Approach To Your Units

Adopting an owner's perspective in overseeing your rental property, we've developed strategies to enhance tenants' experiences, consequently minimizing turnover rates without causing you any inconvenience. Recognizing the financial benefits of reducing preventive maintenance, we prioritize guidelines that help you avoid hefty bills. Our tenants adhere to a stringent set of rules aimed at preventing unnecessary repairs to your property. To uphold these standards, we conduct quarterly inspections of each unit, complemented by bi-weekly drive-bys to assess the exterior of your property, utilizing efficient property management tools in Charlottetown and Summerside.

Tenant Orientation

Our Tenant Orientation, a proven and reliable method, ensures that every tenant comprehensively understands their unit and its proper care. Upon filling a vacancy and welcoming a new tenant, they will be provided with the New Tenant Orientation booklet, a valuable resource. This booklet guides them through essential information about their new home and how to maintain it optimally.

Included in the orientation are crucial details such as locating the main water shut-off valve to mitigate potential water damage in the event of a line burst emergency. Tenants will also learn how to monitor the oil tank level, turn off the main power to the house, and more. Empowering tenants with these skills has proven effective in reducing unnecessary service calls and enhancing their quality of life, ultimately decreasing the likelihood of turnover. Utilizing property management tools in Charlottetown and Summerside further ensures a seamless orientation process.
